Main concept

System overview

The main concept of our system is designed to help university administrators manage and monitor their students’ extracurricular activities through a cross-platform application.

All activity information is centralized in the system, allowing students to easily participate in activities and track their progress. The system automates manual tasks such as data import and export, and provides users with an easy-to-use and user-friendly application.

Our system will keep users updated with the latest information. Users will receive regular updates through website or mobile push notifications to ensure they don't miss important information.

Systems entity overview

Students

Student is the end user of our system, they can take part in the activity and track their accumlation.

Unit

A unit(organization), represents for a group or club, is essentially a collection of users managed by a designated unit administrator.

The unit has the capability to organize and host various activities.

Department

A department, which can represent a faculty or a center, is an entity with the capability to oversee a group of units.

An activity hosted by a unit must receive approval from the associated department before it can go live.

Administrator

This is the most influential user responsible for managing all departments and even handling infrastructure matters.

Administrator can designate specific users as department administrators.

Mobile version

The evolution of mobile technology is rapid, and nearly every user now spends a significant amount of time on their phones.

Figure out this point, we provide a mobile version of A7Y with a user-friendly interface and experience (UI/UX) while retaining core features such as viewing schedules, accumulation tracking, chatbot access, displaying lists of ongoing activities, and suggesting suitable activities for the current user.

Activity & score management

We offer a logical flow for managing the activity life cycle with tight restrictions at each step, ensuring that every activity is handled with care, minimizing unexpected errors, and enhancing overall performance.

Our system serves as a centralized hub for all activities, making it easy for students to access and obtain the latest information.

We also offer real-time accumulation updates, keeping students constantly informed about their progress and enabling them to take appropriate actions.

Automatic call center

Admins can upload a text file containing the school's regulations, and the chatbot will then learn from the content within the file.

When a student posts a question to our chatbot, the system will automatically generate a relevant response based on the context from what the chatbot has learned.

We integrate with Chat-GPT to handle natural language processing, ensuring that the generated questions are generally accurate and easy for students to understand.

The chatbot is available 24/7 and processes inquiries automatically, ensuring students can readily resolve their issues.

Push notifications

Concerned about missing important activities or notifications?

Don't worry, as we also offer push notifications. These notifications will directly inform you of the latest updates related to your user account, such as new suitable activities, updates from your favorite organizations, activity approvals or rejections, new results, and notifications about insufficient accumulation, and more.

Worried about receiving spam? No need to fret, as we offer the option to receive only important notifications based on your preferences.
